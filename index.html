Jetpack: EAC Network Notifier - Version 0.1 (22 May, 2009)

This Jetpack will put a small Confluence icon on the bottom right of your Firefox window.

When you click this icon, it will jump to your network page on EAC. If anyone in your network updates their status, it will show that as a Growl notification - along with their user icon.

This is my first Jetpack test script. Adapted from Mozilla's example and hacked at with bad JavaScript skills late last night. Apologies for butchering the language - help most welcome!
